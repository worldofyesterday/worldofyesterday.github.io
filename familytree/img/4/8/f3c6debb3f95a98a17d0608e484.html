Moses and Katarina Reiss with children by first marriage

Gramps ID O2792
File Type image/jpeg
Date about 1870

Narrative

Labeled by Vera David as follows:
"Moses & Kararina Reiss (around 1870). His children by previous marriage, Julius, Samuel, Paula, Betty"
The photo was mounted as a postcard at the studio Kunst- und Vergrösserungs-Anstalt, A. Wolf, München, Lindwurmstr. 70, Telephone 11989.

The identifications are doubtful since Betty is older than the boys.  It is possible that the taller girl is Betty, the boys are Julius and Samuel, and the younger girl is Irma.
