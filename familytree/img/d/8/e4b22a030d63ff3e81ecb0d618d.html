Aron and Johanna Frisch, Inscription in Hebrew

File Type image/jpeg
Date

Narrative

Honest and pleasant man, pride of his home and sons, the honorable Aharon, son of the honorable Avraham Frisch of blessed memory, from the town of Boskowitz, died at the age of 87, to the great sorrow of his sons and friends, on Tuesday, 9th of Elul, and buried on Wednesday, 10th of Elul, 5656. The great woman, crown of her husband and pride of her sons, Mrs. Hindl, wife or R. Aharon Frisch, died at the age of 81 to the sorrow of her husband and sons, on Wednesday, 27th of Tevet, and buried on Friday, 29th of Tevet, 5655.
