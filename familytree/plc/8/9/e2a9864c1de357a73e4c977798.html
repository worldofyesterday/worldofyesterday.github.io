Narrative

It was formed by the merger of the formerly independent villages of Bradlné, Rozhraní and Vilémov (cadastral Vilémov u Rozhraní) in 1950. The village of Bradlné probably existed as early as 1200 (Brlenka?), Vilémov has been documented since 1409. All three are depicted on Müller's map of Moravia from 1716 (Brallin, Rosrain, Willimow) and I. (1764–1768), II. (1836-1852 - Bradleny, Rossrein, Willimow) and III. (Bradleny, Rossrein / Interface, Willimow) Austrian military mapping.
