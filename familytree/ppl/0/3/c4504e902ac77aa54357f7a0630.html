Event Note

Frantiska Wotzaskova was born in 1897. During the war she was deported with Transport Ca from Praha,Praha Hlavni Mesto,Bohemia,Czechoslovakia to Theresienstadt,Ghetto,Czechoslovakia on 24/10/1942. Deported with transport By from Theresienstadt,Ghetto,Czechoslovakia to Auschwitz Birkenau,Camp on 26/10/1942. Frantiska was murdered/perished in the Shoah. This information is based on a List of Theresienstadt camp inmates found in Terezinska Pametni Kniha/Theresienstaedter Gedenkbuch, Terezinska Iniciativa, vol. I-II Melantrich, Praha 1995, vol. III Academia Verlag, Prag 2000.
