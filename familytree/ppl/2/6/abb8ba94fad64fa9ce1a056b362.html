Narrative

" ... I don't know much about Teddy Blum, I was a child when my Aunt Lizzy Blum came from Prague to Rome (where I grew up) to visit us for the first and only time. She remained a month, and told to my mother the sad story of Teddy. She got a visa to England but he didn't, as he was a physician, and in England they had enough of them. So his fate was done... They were very much in love with each other, and my Aunt never recovered, nor married again. Very sad..." (Cristina Reiss Gigl, 26 Dec 2012)
