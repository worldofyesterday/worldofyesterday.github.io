        HAROLD KOTLER

        TOMS RIVER --  Harold Kotler, a retired sheet metal worker, died yesterday at Community Memorial Hospital, here.

        Mr. Kotler, 62, was born in Brooklyn, N.Y., and lived in Teaneck Township and Newark before moving here 10 years ago.

        He was a 21-year employed member of Sheet Metal Workers Local 28, New York. He retired two years ago.

        Mr. Kotler was a Navy veteran of World War II.

        Surviving are his wie, the former Ruth Schoen; a son, Michael, here; two brothers, Irving, Pembroke, Fla., and Stanley, Brooklyn, and four sisters, Ida Balbier, Deerfield Beach, Fla., Gertrude Kotler and Marilyn Culello, both Far Rockaway, N.Y., and Eleanor Greenberg, East Meadow, N.Y.

        The Silverton Memorial Funeral Home, Dover Township, is in charge of arrangements.
