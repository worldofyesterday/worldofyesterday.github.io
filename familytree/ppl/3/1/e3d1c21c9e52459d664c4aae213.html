Narrative

There are no documents directly linking Eduard, Cäcilie, and Julie as siblings with parents Jacob and Amalie. However, it would require an unusual set of coincidences otherwise. Evidence:
  • Eduard is son of Jakob Frisch who lived in Chrastau (census record). This Jakob was born in Rozhraní.
  • Julie's parents are Jakob Frisch and Amalia of Stvolová (marriage record).
  • Cäcilie was born in Stvolová (death record). Born FRISCH but parents' names not recorded.
  • Cäcilie's daughter Anna was born in Chrastau.
  • In 1854 Amálie Frisch purchased Brandweinhaus number 17 in Stvolová.
