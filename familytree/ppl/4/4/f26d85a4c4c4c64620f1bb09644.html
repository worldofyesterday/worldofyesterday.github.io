Narrative

Egon Weiss owned a 1/8 th share in a house and factory building (the “Erste Wiener Dampfwäscherei A. Weiner”), both located at Dammstrasse 27-29 in
Vienna 20. The records further indicate that the factory had been aryanized. These records further indicate that the Gestapo seized Egon Weiss’ properties.
