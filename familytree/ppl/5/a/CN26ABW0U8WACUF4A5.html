Event Note

November 26, 1937 - June 8, 2013
Tamarac, FL - Formerly of Parksville, NY

Fred M. Gasthalter, the former owner and operator of the Best Western Paramount Hotel in Parksville, NY, and former longtime area resident, died on Saturday, June 8, 2013 in the University of Miami Hospital, Miami, FL. He was 75 years old.

Fred was a member of the Parksville Synagogue, and the Fallsburg Lodge of the Free and Accepted Masons.

He is survived by his sister, Sandra Kata and her husband, Henry of White Plains, NY; his nephews: Dr. Steven Katz and his wife, Lisa and David Katz and his wife, Sheri; his niece, Allyson Schlesinger and her husband, Jarrod. Fred is also survived by his girlfriend, Eleanor Cohen.

Graveside funeral services will be held on Tuesday, June 11th in the Parksville Synagogue Cemetery, Lilly Pond Rd., Parksville, NY; Rabbi Marvin Goldman will officiate. Funeral arrangements by Joseph N. Garlick Funeral Home Inc., Monticello.
