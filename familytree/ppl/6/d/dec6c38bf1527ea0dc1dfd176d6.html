Tichý, Otakar

Birth Name Tichý, Otakar
Gender male
Age at Death 70 years, 1 month, 6 days

Narrative

* August 30, 1906 Okrouhlá u Boskovic - † 5.10.1976 Brno
economic geographer and educator; War and Resistance 1938-1945; a participant in the domestic resistance
nationality: Czech

Czechoslovakia: First Professor of Geography
Came from a teacher's family

Education:
state grammar school in Brno (1925 graduation),
Masaryk University in Brno - the study of natural history and geography,
University of Business in Prague - the study of economic geography (1952 RNDr. 1957 habilitation in Brno 1967 CSc., in 1968, appointed associate professor of theory teaching geography)

work
Publications:
- Economic geography textbook (1947)
- Methodology geography
- Mathematical Geography and Cartography (1965, 1970).

His work is very extensive, counting on 150 titles and publications.

employment:
193-1939 Teacher in Poprad, Banska Bystrica,
1939-1948 Secondary School Professor in České Budějovice, Brno,
1946-1949 Geography Teacher at the Political and Social College in Brno,
1949-1951 Regional School Inspector,
1951-1959 Masaryk's Pedagogical Faculty (1975), Chairman of the Czechoslovak Geographical Society at the Czechoslovak Academy of Sciences in Prague (since 1975), External Lecturer at the Faculty of Education, University of Brno,
1959-1975 Department of Geography of Palacky University in Olomouc (12 years) in Nitra

 

professional and interest organizations
member of the underground Sokol organization Jindra (since 1943),
aid in the treatment of wounded guerrillas units Juraj on Kořenec (1945),
athletics - the javelin nominated wider nomination for the Olympic Games in Berlin (1936),
A homeland Society museum in Olomouc,
Astronomical company

comment:
in his scientific work, he dealt in particular with mathematical geography and cartography
