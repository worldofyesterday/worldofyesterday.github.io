  Narrative

Herrmann Frisch is recorded as the son of Moses Frisch and wife Leni. All records for the remaining children indicate parents Moses Frisch and Elizabeth/Liebe Herrmann. The wedding record in Ivanovice na Hané also names the bride/groom as Moses Frisch and Elizabeth Herrmann.  We have merged these families based on the following evidence:
(1) The land records in fond C 17 show that house 30 was owned by Herrmann before purchase by Simon.  Moises and Liebe inherited from Simon.
(2) Herrmann was born after the marriage of Moises and Liebe. He was the eldest son and got his family number before 1841..
