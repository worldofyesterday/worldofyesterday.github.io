        On application for familiant numbers for Moises Präger and Lazar Frisch

        folio 158,160 - 12.6.1812 - suggestion and protokol of the Judengerichten of Boskovice: They suggested to give the two family numbers 235/253 and 282/306 to two second born sons Moises Präger and Lazar Markus Frisch. They send reports from the normal school and birth certificates to prove the normal age of them and the brides. Their manners are good and there are no single first sons in Boskovice right now who could compare with them. In the protokol we can read some details about the wealth of both men. Lazar Frisch said that he got of his father one half of the Brandweinhaus in Rosrein in value of 3 000 f., 500 f. in corn, 200 in linen and 240 in fleece, 704 and 465 in money. Father of Lazar said that he wants to give whole the Brandweinhaus to Lazar and it is known that he is one of the most wealthy man from Jewish town Boskovice.

        folio 153 - 27.8.1812 - Moises Präger and Markus Frisch paid 53,30 for given family number - 50 is one percent of their wealth and 3 is the tax for the family number

        folio 148 - 30.8.1812 - the regional authority in Brno accepted the suggestion and after paying the tax, informing the synagogue and waiting 6 weeks they can marry their brides, but they have to send the missing birth certificate of Carolina Schwarz, bride of Moises Präger (the name of the bride of Lazar Frisch is never mentioned)

        folio 162 - 10.10.1812 - Boskovice domain administration sent to Brno the birth certificate of Carolina Schwarz and quittances of both the men
        table Empfangtbestattigung: 2.11.1812, for Lazar Frisch Herschl Eisler - Vetter des Lazar Fritsch (some relative)

        folio 159 - 30.1.1813 - quittance that Lazar Frisch paid 107 Gulden familiant tax (another tax?)

        folio 154 - 5.3.1813 - the regional authority in Brno got the  quittances and birth certificate of Sara Schwarz, now they need to pay the wedding percent ot the wealth of the bride Sara Schwarz

        folio 152 - 25.5.1813 - Sara Präger paid the wedding percent 12 f. and now the regional authority in Brno needs to know the wealth of the bride of Lazar Frisch. Boskovice domain administration asked the Opatovice domain administration about Lazar Frisch and his bride because they should be there

        folio 149 - 11.6.1813 - last document is the answer of the Oppatovice domain administration that Lazar Frisch and his bride aren't there and so they cannot write anything about the wealth of the bride

        Commentary (VNK): Lazar Frisch had an older brother (perhaps Moises?). The name of the bride is never mentioned. Second sons could marry if they were wealthy enough and family of Markus Frisch was wealthy. The permissions for the wedding was usually given quite quickly but the process before they really could marry was quite long and complicated and here we don't know the end of it.
