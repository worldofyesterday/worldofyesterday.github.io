Narrative

From "Die nicht mehr sind!":
Ernst, a man of giant stature, was with us in Theresienstadt till October 1944, where he was a heavy worker (had to do hard labour) for three years in the “Spedition” (Spedition is a branch of the Theresienstadt self-administration, a kind of moving company for all that needed to be transported).
