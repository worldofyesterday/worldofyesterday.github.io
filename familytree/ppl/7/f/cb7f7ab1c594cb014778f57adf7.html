        Zarrow, Gloria “Gabrielle” - A.k.a. “the Gift of Gab,” died peacefully at home Sunday, May 3, 2020, surrounded by family at the age of 95. Gabrielle, daughter of Paul and Celia Meisel of Westfield, NJ, was an accomplished pianist, an avid museum goer, animal lover, philanthropist, and artist. Gigi will be remembered for her personalized greeting cards, and her work with the Joint Foundation Support as a grant writer. She’s survived by her loving husband of 71 years, Lawrence; Daughter, Lisa (Alan) Tanguay; Granddaughters, Rebecca and Courtney; Sister, Marilynn Klein; Nephew, Peter Horowitz; and Great-Granddaughter, Sophia. Gabrielle is predeceased by her sister, Irma Horowitz.
