Event Note

Robert Straussberg
Robert Straussberg, 89, of West Orange died Feb. 14, 2014. He lived his entire life in the Oranges.

Dr. Straussberg practiced dentistry for 65 years.

For many years he served as a Major in the National Guard.

He was a member of the New Jersey Dental Association and was a board member of Fuld Neighborhood House.

An avid golfer and tennis player, he enjoyed lifetime membership at Mountain Ridge Country Club, West Caldwell.

He is survived by his wife of 61 years, Anita; three daughters, Nancy (Sam) Silver, Carol (Steven) Tobias, and Barbara (Bill) Salzer; a sister, Elinor; a brother, Arthur; and six grandchildren.

Services were held Feb. 17 with arrangements by Bernheim-Apter-Kreitzman Suburban Funeral Chapel, Livingston.
