Cahn, Harry

Birth Name Cahn, Harry
Gender male

Narrative

From Elias Savada: Harry Cahn was my wife's great grandfather. He died in 1957 and his children were all dead by the end of 1960. Harry's first wife, Adeline Singer, died in 1947, and Harry remarried Rose on 11 Mar 1948 in New Rochelle NY (my wife's home town). at least I believe they were married in New Rochelle as the family did live there. Rose was a member of the Trenton Temple in the Bronx, which is where she met Harry.
