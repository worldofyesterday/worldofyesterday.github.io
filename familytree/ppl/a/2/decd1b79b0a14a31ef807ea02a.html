Narrative

WARNING! Connecting Leopold, Moritz, Max and Ignatz as brothers is a bit speculative. Only Leopold and Moritz are known to be sons of Aron and Resi. My evidence:
(1) Family tree of of C. Lass via her uncle Eric Frisch has brothers Ignatz, Leopold and Max.
(2) We know that Ignatz, Moritz and Leopold were born in Bořitov.
(3) We know that Leopold and Max raised their children in Černa Hora.
(4) Moritz living in Černa Hora at time of marriage.

In Moritz' marriage record, father is "Aron M. Frisch" and mother is "Resi geb Fuchs".
