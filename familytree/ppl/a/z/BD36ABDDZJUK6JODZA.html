Birth Name Jellinek, Cäcilia
Gramps ID I319
Gender female

Narrative

Father: Joseph Jellinek, notion merchant and Familiant Nr. 17 from Gaya, house Nr. 73, marriage permittion from 27.2.1840, son of Isak Jellínek from Gaya and Cäcilia Weiss from Prosnitz
Mother: Amalia Placzek, daughter of Moises Placzek, familiant from Gaya and Lea /Leni/ nee Israel Boxhorn from Gaya
Child: Cäcilia, born 28.2.1852 in the house Nr. 73 at Gaya

Narrative

Age in marriage record (26y, 8mo) is consistent with 1852 birth. However, death records (0241/0967/0013) record Sali Jellinek, daughter of Joseph, died Jun 1852 at age 4 months.
