Event Note

NY Times, May 18, 2000:

LEBER-Leopold, M.D. On May 16, 2000. Husband of Edith Rosen Leber. Father of Dr. George B. and Yvonne Leber, Bernice K. Leber and David Rosenberg. Grandfather of Andrew and Melissa Leber and Aaron Rosenberg. Brother of Dr. Arthur and Lusia Leber. Uncle of Dr. Mark Leber. He and Edith survived the Holocaust. He practiced medicine for 35 years in Schenectady, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Englewood Hospital or the Jewish National Fund. Services Thursday 1PM, ''Parkside'' Chapels, Queens Boulevard at 66 Ave, Forest Hills
