Narrative

First lieutenant Military Intelligence, United States Army, 1954-1956.; Member Metropolitan Opera Guild (patron), University of Pennsylvania of L.I.Club, Wharton Business School of New York; Club, Wharton Business; Club of Long Island (board of directors).

Sales representative Foods division Lever Brothers Company, 1956-1958. Vice president marketing sales Jolly Toys Inc., New York City, 1958-1969. Executive vice president Association Industrial Designers Ltd., New York City, 1969-1973.

President Dicker International Inc., New York City, 1973-1993, also subsidiary Ozen Sound Devices Inc., New York City, Tokyo. president New York Times Youth Forums Alumni, 1948-1950, Brichta Assocs., 1993. Association trustee North Shore University Hospital, Manhasset, New York, 1994. Board of Governors General Alumni Society University of Pennsylvania, Philadelphia, 1994.
