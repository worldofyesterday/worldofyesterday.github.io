Narrative

First lieutenant Military Intelligence, United States Army, 1954-1956.; Member Metropolitan Opera Guild (patron), University of Pennsylvania of L.I.Club, Wharton Business School of New York; Club, Wharton Business; Club of Long Island (board of directors).
