Narrative

Grouping of Aron M Frisch and Jakob Frisch as brothers is somewhat conjectural.
  • In the cemetery records, both are "ben Meir," not "ben Meir Löbl."
  • They are close in birth year.
It is clear that the "Aron M" in the cemetery records matches our other records for this person, but conjectural that Jakob *1827 is the same person as Jakob +1896. Jakob *1827 appears with Eva in the 1869 census. Jakob +1896 is buried in section 11, row 1, whereas Sheva (presumed to be Eva, but uncertain) is in section 12, row 1.
