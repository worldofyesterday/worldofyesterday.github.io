i found that you were right about salomon and marie freund. In my

Email from Herbert Freund 31.12.2000:
berthold freund and his wife frieda fany freund (born rybner 16.9.1887
in mahrich ostrau) were deported from theresianstadt to auschwitz on
15.12.43. i found a few red-cross post cards sent by them from
theresianstadt.
