Narrative

Birth records of Salomon Jellinek (1073/932/3) and Joseph Jellinek (932/10) describe Sali Jellinek as daughter of "Michael Freund aus Drewekau in Böhmen" and Nanni Frisch "aus Boskowitz".

Drewekau is presumably Drevikov in Region Chrudim.

In other cases, such as birth record of Charlotte Jellinek (932/61), Michael Freund is "aus Kosteletz".
