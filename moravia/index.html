Jews of Moravia: A Small Library

Hodonín (Göding)

  • Jewish heads of households, 1900 census of Hodonín.
  • Directory of retail shops in Hodonín, 1924. Spreadsheet transcription allows for sorting by name, address, trade.
  • Map of Hodonín, 1920. Useful in conjunction with the retail directory.
  • Map of Hodonín, 1850.
  • Map of Hodonín, 1827.
  • Jahres-Bericht der Deutschen Communal-Realschule in Göding veröffentlicht am Schlusse des Schuljahres, 1911-1912.
    School yearbook has list of donors on pages 88-89 and students on pages 114-115.

Kyjov (Gaya) and surrounding villages

  • Index to Familianten records (Excel format).
  • Jewish heads of households, 1869 census of Kyjov-předměstí.
  • Jewish heads of households, 1880 census of Kyjov-Židé.
  • Jewish heads of households, 1857 census of Kostelec.
  • Jewish heads of households, 1880 census of Lovčice.
  • Jewish heads of households, 1857 census of Nětčice.
  • Jewish heads of households, 1869 census of Nětčice.
  • 1857 census of Svatobořice: No Jewish families found.
  • Jewish heads of households, 1869 census of Svatobořice.
  • Jewish heads of households, 1880 census of Svatobořice.
  • Jewish heads of households, 1880 census of Žádovice.

Strážnice (Strassnitz)

  • Jewish community of Strážnice, 1827. This document includes a list of Jewish heads of households and a color map showing the location of the homes.
  • The Siebenschein Family from Moravia. A well-documented history of a prominent Jewish family of Strážnice.
  • Casino-Verein of Strassnitz. A short document on a social club for the German-speaking citizens of Strážnice.
  • Jewish heads of households, 1890 census of Rohatec.

Třebíč (Trebitsch)

  • Jewish inhabitants, 1857 census of Třebíč-Židovské město.

Uherský Brod (Ung. Brod)

  • Jewish heads of households, 1869 census of Uherský Brod-Židovská obec.
