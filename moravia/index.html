Jews of Moravia: A Small Library

[Hodonín] [Strážnice]

Hodonín (Göding)

  • Directory of retail shops in Hodonín, 1924. Spreadsheet transcription allows for sorting by name, address, trade.
  • Map of Hodonín, 1920. Useful in conjunction with the retail directory.
  • Map of Hodonín, 1850.
  • Map of Hodonín, 1827.
  • Jahres-Bericht der Deutschen Communal-Realschule in Göding veröffentlicht am Schlusse des Schuljahres, 1911-1912. (external link) School yearbook has list of donors on pages 88-89 and students on pages 114-115.

Strážnice (Strassnitz)

  • Jewish community of Strážnice, 1827. This document includes a list of Jewish heads of households and a color map showing the location of the homes.
  • The Siebenschein Family from Moravia. A well-documented history of a prominent Jewish family of Strážnice.
  • Casino-Verein of Strassnitz. A short document on a social club for the German-speaking citizens of Strážnice.
